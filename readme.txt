Add an AI-powered chat widget to your WordPress or WooCommerce store. Help customers get instant answers 24/7.

== Description ==

Taros is an AI-powered chat widget that helps your website visitors get instant answers to their questions. Perfect for WooCommerce stores, blogs, and business websites.

**Features:**

* Easy setup - just enter your Bot ID
* AI-powered responses trained on your content
* Works with any WordPress theme
* WooCommerce compatible
* Mobile responsive
* Customizable appearance
* No coding required

**How it works:**

1. Create a free account at [taros.ai](https://taros.ai)
2. Train your chatbot with your website content
3. Install this plugin and enter your Bot ID
4. Your AI chat widget is live!

**Why Taros?**

* **24/7 Support** - Your chatbot never sleeps
* **Reduce Support Tickets** - Answer common questions automatically
* **Increase Sales** - Help customers find what they need faster
* **Easy Training** - Just point to your website and Taros learns your content

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/taros-chat-widget` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Go to Settings â†’ Taros Chat to configure the plugin.
4. Enter your Bot ID from your [Taros Dashboard](https://app.taros.ai/dashboard).
5. Save settings and the widget will appear on your site!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get a Bot ID? =

Sign up for a free account at [taros.ai](https://taros.ai), create a chatbot, and you'll find your Bot ID in the Widget settings section of your dashboard.

= Does this work with WooCommerce? =

Yes! Taros works great with WooCommerce stores. Your chatbot can help customers find products, answer questions about shipping and returns, and more.

= Can I customize the appearance? =

Yes, you can customize colors, position, and other settings from your Taros dashboard. The widget will automatically use your configured styles.

= Is it mobile friendly? =

Yes, the chat widget is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices.

= Does it affect site speed? =

The widget script loads asynchronously and won't block your page from loading. It's optimized for performance.
